If you are in immediate housing crisis

If you are facing an active eviction today or this week, please also contact:

  • 2-1-1 (United Way helpline — available 24/7 in most areas)
  • Your local legal aid organization — many tenants have legal defenses to eviction
  • The Salvation Army, Catholic Charities, or your local faith-based aid network

Our small board cannot guarantee same-day response. The resources above may be faster for emergencies.
