Governance

HOUSE, Inc. is governed by a volunteer board of directors that meets regularly to:

  • Review and decide on assistance applications
  • Set program policy and eligibility criteria
  • Review financial statements and approve the annual filing
  • Plan fundraising and outreach

Board members serve without compensation. See our Board & Governance page for more information.

How donations are used

HOUSE, Inc. is a small organization with a volunteer board. The largest practical share of every donation is directed to families in the form of direct rental assistance paid to landlords and housing providers.

Because the organization is small and largely volunteer-staffed, administrative overhead is minimal. Donations are not used to pay board members, contractors, or staff salaries except as required for legitimate operational expenses (e.g., accounting, tax preparation, filing fees, banking costs).

Designated gifts are applied to the purpose you specify. If a designation is fully funded, the remainder is redirected to the area of greatest need unless you specify otherwise.

Form 990 filings

HOUSE, Inc. files an annual information return with the IRS:

  • Form 990-N (e-Postcard) — used in years when gross receipts are at or below the IRS threshold for small tax-exempt organizations

Form 990 filings are publicly available through the IRS and through third-party nonprofit databases. We will also provide copies of recent filings on written request.

Records and inspection

As a 501(c)(3) organization, HOUSE, Inc. makes its application for tax-exempt status, annual information returns, and certain governing documents available for public inspection on request. To request documents, email info@houseinc501c3.com with the specific document or information you are seeking.

Donor privacy

We do not sell, rent, or trade donor information. Donor contact information is used only to:

  • Send tax receipts and acknowledgments
  • Communicate organization news and impact updates (you may opt out at any time)
  • Comply with applicable legal and reporting requirements

Concerns or complaints

If you have a question or concern about how HOUSE, Inc. operates, please contact us at info@houseinc501c3.com. We take all concerns seriously and respond in writing.

Concerns about a 501(c)(3) organization may also be directed to the IRS Tax Exempt and Government Entities Division.
