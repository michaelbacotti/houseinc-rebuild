Emergency Housing Assistance Program — Currently Paused

Program on hold

Unfortunately, HOUSE, Inc.'s Emergency Housing Assistance Program is currently on hold due to lack of resources at this time. We are a very small charity with less than a handful of volunteers and little donations received.

Responses via phone and email will be delayed. Email is favored over phone. When the situation improves, we will make an announcement and reopen the program.

Sorry for the inconvenience.

Other resources

While our program is paused, please consider reaching out to:

  • Your local 2-1-1 (United Way helpline) for emergency assistance in your area
  • Your state or county emergency rental assistance program
  • A local legal aid organization if you have received an eviction notice — many tenants have defenses they don't know about
