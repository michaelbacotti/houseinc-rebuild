Emergency Housing Assistance Program — Currently Paused

Program temporarily on hold

HOUSE, Inc.'s Emergency Housing Assistance Program is temporarily paused due to limited resources at this time. We are a small charity with a very limited number of volunteers and modest donation levels, which affects our capacity to accept new applications.

Response times via phone and email will be significantly delayed, and email is preferred whenever possible. When the situation improves, we will make an announcement and reopen the program.

We apologize for the inconvenience and appreciate your patience and understanding.

Other resources

While our program is paused, please consider reaching out to:

  • Your local 2-1-1 (United Way helpline) for emergency assistance in your area
  • Your state or county emergency rental assistance program
  • A local legal aid organization if you have received an eviction notice — many tenants have defenses they don't know about
